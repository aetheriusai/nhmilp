Stimulates New Blood Vessel Growth

Angiogenesis support—designed to assist with formation of new capillaries in underperfused tissues.

May support tissue repair signalling and collagen synthesis involved in wound healing.

Improves Immune Response

Designed to assist with immune function and cellular defense mechanisms.

Stem Cell Mobilization

May help promote mobilization of stem cells from bone marrow to support tissue repair.
