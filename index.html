Study Eligibility

The following criteria are intended to assist with preliminary self-qualification. Final eligibility determination is made by the study physician.

Inclusion Criteria

  • • Adults aged 18-75 years seeking investigational hyperbaric oxygen therapy
  • • Ability to commit to 5 sessions per week for 12 consecutive weeks
  • • Medical clearance from primary care physician or specialist
  • • Interest in areas of cognitive function, systemic inflammation, tissue recovery, or cellular optimization research
  • • No contraindications to hyperbaric oxygen therapy as determined by study physician

Exclusion Criteria

  • • Active untreated pneumothorax or severe pulmonary conditions
  • • Uncontrolled claustrophobia or inability to tolerate chamber environment
  • • Pregnancy or planning pregnancy during study period
  • • Certain medications or medical devices that may contraindicate HBOT
  • • Inability to provide informed consent or complete study requirements

Final eligibility is determined by the study physician during screening.

Study Focus Areas

This investigational study is designed to assist with research in the following areas:

Cognitive Function

Researching the impact of high-pressure oxygen on neuro-inflammation and executive function markers.

Systemic Response

Assessing biological markers of inflammation and oxidative stress over a 60-session period.

Tissue Recovery

Evaluating healing acceleration associated with complex soft-tissue or post-surgical recovery needs.

Cellular Optimization

Investigating cellular senescence and mitochondrial health through structured hyper-oxygenation protocols.
